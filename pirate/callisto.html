Callisto

Schmaiden Schmelvin is a kid with four bunnies named Hoppy McHoppersons, Jumposaurus Rex, Cinnabun, and Paul Bunnyan. The bunnies each have a super snuggly blanket with their favorite delicious snacks (carrots, radishes, bok choy, brussels sprouts) on it ! Every afternoon Schmaidan makes sure to tuck each bunny under their special blankie and make sure they get just the right length nap. Use the clues below to figure out which bunny gets which blankie, and how long to set their alarm clocks.

  1. Hoppy McHoppersons naps 20 minutes longer than the bunny with the carrot blanket.
  2. The bunny that naps 40 minutes likes bok choy.
  3. Cinnabun likes a treat that starts with B.
  4. Jumpasaurus Rex naps under a carrot blanket for more than 30 minutes.
  5. Paul Bunnyan naps under a blanket with bok choy on it.
